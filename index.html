Your Land, Our Expertise

MN Dichoso Land Surveying Service is led by licensed Geodetic Engineer Malaika N. Dichoso, with an office at Pongco, Poblacion, Bacon District, Sorsogon City. Founded in 2023 after 13 years in the mining industry, the company brings expert knowledge and a passion for serving the local community. We provide precise, reliable, and professional surveying services, ensuring your land is handled with the care and accuracy it deserves.
